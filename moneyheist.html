TV Series. Thriller. Mystery. Action | Heist Film. Kidnapping Film / Disappearance

TV Series (2017-2021). 5 Seasons. A mysterious character called the Professor is planning the biggest robbery in history. To do this, he recruits eight thieves who take hostages and lock themselves in the Royal Mint of Spain as The Professor manipulates the police to carry out his plan from the outside. This group of very peculiar robbers have a plan: assault the Factory of Moneda and Timbre to carry out the most perfect robbery in the history of Spain and taking home 2,400 million euros.
